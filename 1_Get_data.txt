Get the following datasets to run the analyses:

#### Environmental data folder:

LUI_standardized_global.txt (Open Access)
Land-use intensity from Bexis LUI tool: https://www.bexis.uni-jena.de/lui/LUICalculation/index
Options: Year 2008-2017; standardised, global.

31018_5_Dataset/31018_5_data.xslx (Not available yet)
Aggregated environmental and land use covariates of grassland EPs
https://www.bexis.uni-jena.de/ddm/data/Showdata/31018
